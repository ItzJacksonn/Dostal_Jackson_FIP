5 - Star Restaurants

The Keg Steakhouse

At an almost perfect rating, this steakhouse serves both classic steak and seafood. They offer dine-in, as well as take out. This premuim restaurant does come with a premuim price tag!

Zander's Fire Grill

A great hangout spot with wonderful food. Zander's serves a vareity of food from burgers and fries to spaghetti and pasta. This restuarant is the perfect place to take the family out of the house!

4 - Star Restaurants

Montanas's

With a rating of 4.1 stars, Montana's is a great family restaurant to dine at. This restaurant has a main focus on BBQ style meals that range from wings to ribs. Dine-in and take-out are both availible. You can also have your food delivered directly to your house if that works better for you!

Myra's Grill and Bar

There are many ways to enjoy Myra's Grill and Bar whether it be through their dine-in or take-out services. The wide vareity that myra's offers will ensure that everyone can enjoy a memorable meal!

3 - Star Restaurants

Swiss Chalet

A great family restaurant that can serve people of all ages. The best menu that a chain restaurant can offer. This restuarant has a vast amount of options to choose from. They offer anything from pizz to steak. Large groups can be easily served, so this place is great for parties!

Williams Fresh Cafe

With a rating of 3.8 stars, this cafe is an execllent stop for a nice cup of coffee or a great stop for a din-in breakfast which is also served here. A drive-through option makes Williams Fresh Cafe a great idea to grab a snack for a long drive!
