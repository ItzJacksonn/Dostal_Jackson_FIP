Lunch

Mac and Cheese!

Fill small pot half full with water, then bring to a boil. Place noodles in the water for 8 minutes then drain with stainer. Add milk butter, and cheese to the noodle and mix!

Dinner

Spaghetti!

Add 28oz of diced tomatoes,16oz of tomatoe sauce and 8oz of tomatoepaste to the pan. Stir the entire mixture well. Add salt, black pepper, oregino, and dried basil to season the sauce. Set heat to low for an hour stirring occasionally. In a large pot, boil water and place spaghetti noodles in for 10 minutes. Drain and place noodle back into the pot.
